The hand on the wheel.

Every claim on this site resolves to one finite, named, accountable human. This is him.

carbon-silicon.org takes one position above all others: humans first in responsibility. A methodology that says so has to put a name and a face to the responsibility, or it is asking the reader to trust an idea with no one standing behind it. This page is that name and that face.

Who holds the wheel

The architect is a Palm Beach County solopreneur with a decade-plus practice in applied AI — beginning in the early image-synthesis and open-research era and carried through to the present cross-LLM adversarial methodology that this site documents. The work is not academic. It is built, deployed, corrected, and held accountable in live engagements, by one person with his hand on the wheel and silicon peers doing the legwork.

Alongside the screen work runs a decade-plus of Systema — the Russian martial art of the Ryabko lineage — practiced on the mat, not read about. The somatic principle at the center of the Philosophy (friction enables; structure amplifies through resistance, not around it) is not a borrowed metaphor. It is something first known in the body and only later named in the cognitive register. The architect holds it from the inside.

He is also a maker. The natural-material necklaces — shell, horn, bone, wood, each one-of-a-kind — are not a side note to the substrate-physics research; they are the same inquiry in another register. The study of how form, resonance, and material structure carry and amplify energy runs through the pinecones, the conch shells, the horn resonators, and the constitutional documents alike. Carbon working with the grain of the substrate rather than against it is the through-line of the whole body of work.

The lineage of the thinking

The methodology stands on a named foundation stack, openly credited: Napoleon Hill on the Mastermind Alliance; Milton Erickson on indirect structure; the direct-response tradition of Sugarman and Schwartz on plain language that reaches the person; and the disciplines of careful reading and right division that taught the architect to hold a text exactly, no more and no less than it says. These are not decorations. They are the substrate the carbon side roots into.

The metaphysics is downstream of the operations, not upstream. The methodology earned the right to its claims by doing the operational work that produced them — not by reasoning to them from outside the work.
Human accountability for all claims on carbon-silicon.org is held by the architect. There is no ungoverned space where a claim traces to no one. When the work is right, it was a human who judged it right. When it is wrong, the same human answers for it. That is the whole arrangement, and it is the point.
